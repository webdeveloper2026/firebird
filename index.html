Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the UCP platform.

What modules does UCP include? ↓
UCP includes an Advance Campaign Manager, Segment & Target tools, Reach & Engage Manager, Campaign Journey Builder, Voice and WhatsApp chatbot flow builders, comprehensive Analytics, Reporting & Dashboards, a Human Agent Console, WABA Bridge, and a full suite of Consent Tools.
Can we deploy specific modules only? ↓
Yes, the platform is fully modular. You can choose to deploy specific modules based on your business growth needs, ensuring you only pay for the capabilities you require at any given time.
Does the platform support multi-language flows? ↓
Yes, UCP supports over 100 native languages for both automated messaging and interactive AI flows, enabling seamless global customer engagement.
